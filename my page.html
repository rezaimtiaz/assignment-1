BEAUTY OF COXSBAZAR

Stretching about 120 km, it’s recognized as the longest unbroken sandy sea beach on earth.Its gently sloping, golden sands are perfect for long walks, jogging, or watching sunrise and sunset.The scenic Cox’s Bazar–Teknaf Marine Drive covers 80 km along cliffs and shoreline, offering breathtaking ocean and hill views.Nearby Inani Beach boasts clear waters and coral-stone formations, making it ideal for snorkeling.Framed by lush hills and tropical forests to the north and south, the region invites exploration beyond the beach.
